Carbonara

Description

This dish is absolutely fit and just out of this world in terms of taste and perfection. Once the mouth catches just a drop off delight from this heavenly dish your urge to want more of it persists until, you can no longer stomach anymore.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
    • \
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • half a cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 clove garlic minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 8 slices bacon, diced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound of spaghetti

Steps

  1. Step 1
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti in boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes. Drain, toss spaghetti with 1 tablespoon olive oil, and set aside.
  3. Step 2
  4. Place diced bacon in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain bacon on paper towels, reserving 2 tablespoons bacon fat in the skillet.
  5. Step 3
  6. Add 1 tablespoon olive oil to bacon fat in the skillet. Add chopped onion and cook over medium heat until onion is translucent. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add wine and cook 1 minute more.
  7. Step 4
  8. Return cooked bacon to the skillet; add cooked spaghetti. Toss to coat and heat through, adding more olive oil if it seems dry or sticks together. Add beaten eggs and cook, tossing constantly with tongs or a large fork, until eggs are barely set. Quickly add 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese and toss again. Season with salt and pepper (remember that bacon and Parmesan are very salty).
  9. Step 5
  10. Serve warm with chopped parsley sprinkled on top and extra Parmesan cheese at the table.
