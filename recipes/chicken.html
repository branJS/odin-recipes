Chicken Fried Chicken

Description

Chicken Fried Chicken. Where could we go wrong. Calling all chicken lovers to dive into this absolutely iconic recipe. Leaving the creator of this dish feeling powerful. Succulent cripsy texture around the chicken to raise your taste buds to a place they've never been before.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons dry potato flakes
  • 1 teaspoon seasoned salt
  • half a teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 cups of vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Step 1
  2. Place crackers in a large resealable plastic bag; seal bag and crush crackers with a rolling pin until they are coarse crumbs. Add the flour, potato flakes, seasoned salt, and pepper and mix well.
  3. Step 2
  4. Beat egg in a shallow dish or bowl. One by one, dredge chicken pieces in egg, then place in bag with crumb mixture. Seal bag and shake to coat.
  5. Step 3
  6. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  7. Step 4
  8. Fry chicken, turning frequently, until golden brown and juices run clear, 15 to 20 minutes.
